Rules

How to Make Your Picks

1Go to the Group Stage tab — you'll see all 72 group stage matches listed by date.
2For each match, tap Home Win, Draw, or Away Win to make your result pick.
3Optionally enter a predicted score (e.g. 2–1) in the score boxes below each match. This is how you earn the exact score bonus.
4When you're done, hit the Save Picks button at the bottom of the screen. Your picks are not saved until you tap that button.
5You can come back and change any pick right up until that specific match kicks off. Once a match locks you cannot change it — but all other upcoming matches stay open.
6For the Knockout Stage, team matchups will be announced once the group stage is complete. Check back after June 27th to make your knockout picks.

Scoring System

+3Correct result in any match — group stage or knockout stage.
+1Correct draw prediction in a group stage match.
+2Exact score bonus — awarded when your predicted scoreline matches the official final score exactly (e.g. you pick 2–1 and it ends 2–1). You must have also picked the correct result. Exact score bonuses are reviewed and awarded by the admin once the final game of each day has been played.
+0Wrong guess — no points awarded.

Tiebreaker Rules

1stClosest guess to the total goals scored in the entire tournament (all 104 matches combined).
2ndClosest guess to the total goals scored in the Final match only.
3rdWhoever registered earliest wins. This is automatic — no action needed.

Important Reminders

🔒Each match locks at its official kickoff time. Make your picks early — no pick = 0 points.
📧You'll receive an email reminder if you have unpicked matches kicking off within the next 24 hours — sent daily at 9:00 AM ET.
🏆Knockout stage matchups will be announced after the group stage ends on June 27th. Keep an eye out!
